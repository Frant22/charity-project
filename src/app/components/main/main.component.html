The practical activity of the Correctional Center was launched in 2011, and the public organization "Our Childhood" was created on April 15, 2013 in Donetsk to provide assistance to families with disabled children (autism, mental retardation, Down syndrome, Williams syndrome, Rubinstein syndrome, hydrocephalus) From 2011 to 2014, the Center provided assistance to 200 families, and the rented area increased from 15 meters to 200 meters in 2 districts of the city of Donetsk.

The events of 2014 in the East forced the families who studied at the Our Childhood Center to leave Donetsk. Some families moved to Kyiv. Public organization "Our Childhood" was re-registered on February 12, 2015 in Kyiv. From 2013 to the present, the Center has provided assistance to 400 children.

"Our Childhood", today, is one of the leading institutions working with this category of children. Our Childhood works with children from the age of 1, the oldest student is 20 years old. Our Childhood works with families from Israel, Italy, Spain, Belarus, Germany, and Denmark. Also, families from other cities of Ukraine (Nikolaev, Kropyvnytskyi, Lviv, Krivoy Rog, Mariupol, Dnipro, Kherson, Odessa, Kharkov) constantly come.

Our Childhood employs specialists from different areas of work:
The best indicator of the work of the center is the results of our children. Our children have the best reading skills in their classes, participate in math olympiads, play the piano and participate in concerts. In "Our Childhood" they know how to teach mentally retarded children to read and write. The use of the latest teaching methods allows most children to adapt to life in society, to acquire the necessary skills for effective interaction with the environment.

The above results were made possible thanks to carefully selected techniques:

ABA-therapy (analysis and behavior correction);

mnemonics (an associative way of remembering letters, numbers and other information);

Author's methods of Mantulina (teaching mathematics and reading, writing);

Sensory integration;

To fulfill these statutory tasks, the following were organized on the basis of the Civil Defense:

- group classes in creativity and music, where children learn to work in a group of peers (groups include children with different nosologies (autism, cerebral palsy, mental retardation, Down syndrome, Rubinstein syndrome));

-mini communication groups, where children with autism learn to communicate, maintain a conversation and acquire social skills in relation to age;

-individual classes, which are conducted according to the personal program of the child and are aimed at intellectual, speech, fine motor development;

-speech therapy classes in which children learn to speak and overcome the shortcomings of the language;

-classes on neurocorrection, in which children learn to perform physical exercises aimed at overcoming sensory difficulties.

-family holidays where children learn to perform on stage (sing, dance). The events are aimed at creating a healthy atmosphere in families raising a disabled child.

-classes with an audiologist provide an opportunity for children who are unable to speak to receive an alternative communication system (sign language);

-consultations for parents, where they receive useful information regarding the organization of the child's life, developing space at home, consolidating the skills acquired in the classroom.
